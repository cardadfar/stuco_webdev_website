98-331: Student-Taught Course | Animation & Video Editing

Close All

What experience is required to take the course?

No prior knowledge of art is assumed. Students with drawing experience may find the animation unit easier. Students without drawing experience can use the animation unit as means of gaining drawing experience while learning animation concepts & strategies.

I don't have access to any of the Adobe software on my laptop?

CFA rooms 317 & 318 come loaded with the Adobe Creative Suite. Adobe also offers 7-day free trials for all their software. For those who need Adobe software locally on their computer, Adobe finances all their software for a flat montly rate that is discouted for students.

I am skilled already in [insert name of non-adobe software here]. Can I do my work in this software?

For the most part, yes. The course will still be taught using the Adobe Creative Suite. Some units are more specific to a certain Adobe software, but unless otherwise specified, you are free to use any software.

How are assignments graded?

Assignments are graded based on completion. The objective of an assignment is to demonstrate a proper understanding topics covered within the last week. It should be evident that you spent at least 30 minutes working on the assignment.

Am I allowed to use copyrighted audio/visuals in my assignments?

Yes. Be sure to give credit where credit is due. If you use copyrighted music, the best place to put the credits is at the end of the video for a short duration that way it stays with the video.

Are there late days for assignments?

Due dates for the most part will not be inforced. It is in a student's best interest to complete the weekly assignments in a timely manner so that the work doesn't pile up. The Midterm & Final will have a more strict deadline.

I know I won't be in class on a given day. Should I let you know?

Don't worry about emailing me. Students can look at the syllabus to see what topics will be covered that day and use YouTube, Lynda.com, & other resources to research the topics in order to complete the weekly 'quick-edit' on their own.

I am really busy this week and cannot complete my assignment. What should I do?

A StuCo should be the last of your worries. Take a late-day, or a late-week. You are allowed to skip 2 assignments (not including the Midterm & Final).

When are office hours?

Office hours are by email appointment with 24 hours notice. Any request made in less than 24 hours cannot be guaranteed.
